The Heritage of the Glass

The Smoked Amber is more than a cocktail; it is a ritual. Originating from the private ateliers of the 1920s, this particular variation utilizes a cold-smoke technique with aged hickory to elevate the inherent caramel notes of single-barrel bourbon.

The addition of hand-crafted cedarwood bitters bridges the gap between the smokiness and the bright zest of charred orange peel, creating a multi-sensory experience that evolves as the ice slowly yields to the spirit.

The Preparation

01

The Infusion

Cold smoke the mixing glass using hickory wood chips for exactly 30 seconds to capture the essence without bitterness.

02

The Blend

Combine 2oz Bourbon, 1 barspoon of maple syrup, and 3 dashes of cedar bitters. Stir over large-format ice for 45 rotations.

03

The Garnish

Express charred orange oils over the rim and drop into the glass. Serve immediately while the smoke still clings to the crystal.

restaurant_menu Curated Ingredients
